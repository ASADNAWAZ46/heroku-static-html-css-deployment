INTRODUCTION

MCS enjoys the reputation of being one of the top engineering consultancy organizations in Pakistan. MCS was established in 2020 as a private limited company by the Government of Pakistan. The objective of its creation was to create a pool of talented engineers, attain self-reliance in engineering consultancy and replace foreign consultants. Currently MCS has total strength of over 40+ employees.
The Total estimated turnover for the year 2020 was Rs. 8 Million with the total cumulative cost of the projects undertaken by MCS is 30 Million.
